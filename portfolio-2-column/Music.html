Why Music?

I love music. The rich sound of Violin, Violas, Veenas, the amazing sound of a voice, the great beats of percussion, I love music. It draws me. It enraptures me. It captivates me.

CCC+ Carnatic singing

CCC stands for Carnatic chamber of Concerts. I have been on there two times so far, each one for ten minutes. CCC is a lace where young kinds can showcase either accompanying or singing in the carnatic classical style of music.
